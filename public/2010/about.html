RubyConf India 2010

India's first RubyConf is happening in Bangalore in March 2010. RubyConf India is presented by the Ruby Community in India and supported by RubyCentral and the Innovation & Technology Trust.
Royal Orchid Hotel, Bangalore
20th & 21st March

About

The Ruby Community in India together with the Innovation & Technology Trust is pleased to announce RubyConf India 2010 on Mar 20th and 21st at the Royal Orchid Hotel, Bangalore. Targeted at Ruby practitioners and anyone remotely interested or curious about Ruby, it will offer Ruby enthusiasts an opportunity brainstorm, network and learn about the latest technology changes first-hand.

Being the first ever RubyConf to be hosted in India, this 2-day event will feature prominent speakers like Yukihiro “Matz” Matsumoto (the creator of Ruby himself will deliver a talk via video-conference), Chad Fowler author of “The Passionate Programmer” and “My Job went to India”, Ola Bini core-committer to JRuby since 2006, Obie Fernandez author of “The Rails Way” and Nick Sieger of Engine Yard and JRuby fame.

Ruby is an OS dynamic language that offers an ideal development environment for Agile practitioners. It has one of the most active open source communities worldwide which produces and supports tools and projects like “Ruby on Rails” - a powerful web application development framework that works well on different platforms, and significantly reduces time-to-market and operating costs.

A strong supporter of emerging OS technologies that promise to revolutionize the IT landscape, ThoughtWorks is the Platinum and Technology Sponsor for this event and is supported by Ruby Central – an international non-profit dedicated to the advocacy of the Ruby programming language across the globe. Ecosystem support includes Gold Sponsors Castle Rock Research Corporation & Hashrocket, Silver Sponsor Mahaswami Software & Aditya Institutions and Media Partner Silicon India. Register today at www.rubyconfindia.org!
